Transforming Statespace/Aimlab


Overview:

In my previous role as the Head of Design at Statespace, I spearheaded transformative initiatives that revolutionized performance analytics in the gaming and esports industry. Leveraging my expertise in product design and UX methodologies, I played a pivotal role in propelling Statespace to the forefront of cognitive science and artificial intelligence-driven improvement. This comprehensive project breakdown showcases my significant contributions and achievements, highlighting my design leadership and successful collaborations with renowned industry partners.

Elevating Performance Analytics through Innovative Tools:

At Statespace, our mission was to empower casual gamers and esports athletes to maximize their potential through the development of cutting-edge tools. By integrating cognitive science and artificial intelligence, we introduced standardized performance analytics that provided users with invaluable insights and actionable data to enhance their gaming skills and overall performance.

Expanding User Base and Strategic Partnerships:

During my tenure, Aim Lab, our flagship product, experienced unprecedented growth, with the user base skyrocketing to an impressive 5 million monthly active users and 20 million total players. This remarkable increase in engagement and adoption reflects our unwavering commitment to delivering exceptional user experiences and staying at the forefront of industry trends.

Additionally, I successfully directed creative initiatives that resulted in notable intellectual property partnerships with industry giants such as Riot Games, Ubisoft, and Call of Duty. Through these strategic collaborations, we seamlessly integrated our performance analytics tools into highly popular games such as Valorant, Arcane, League of Legends, and Rainbow Six Siege, enhancing the gaming experience for millions of players.

Fundraising Success and Investor Confidence: Our groundbreaking vision and achievements also garnered significant investor confidence, leading to successful fundraising efforts. To date, we secured over $98 million in funding from reputable investors, including Khosla Ventures, Lux Capital, Bessemer Venture Partners, Modern Venture Partners, Mirae Asset, Animal Capital, Riverside, Gaingels, and others. This strong backing not only validates our disruptive impact within the gaming and esports landscape but also fuels our drive to push boundaries and pioneer new frontiers.

Conclusion:

As the Head of Design, I played a pivotal role in driving Statespace's success and establishing its leadership position in performance analytics. By prioritizing user-centered design, leveraging cutting-edge technologies, and passionately transforming the gaming industry, I continuously elevated user experiences, expanded our user base, and forged strategic collaborations with renowned IP partners.

I take great pride in showcasing my expertise in product design and UX methodologies, which have contributed to shaping the future of performance analytics and empowering gamers worldwide to unleash their full potential.
